TeamBotsTM Domain: ForageAAAI97

Forage behaviors used in 1997 at AAAI

    Two simulated Nomad 150 robots search for objects to collect and return to the homebase (center of the field). In this case, the orange objects must be delivered to a location in front of the orange bin (simulated by a big orange circle). Blue objects must be delivered to the blue bin. There are two types of objects to collect: moving simulated squiggle balls and stationary blue balls. It is very hard to catch the squiggle balls. This is the actual code used by Georgia Tech in the 1997 competition.
